Introduction to E-Tech

Empowerment Technology (E-Tech) is designed to help students and educators harness digital tools for communication, innovation, and collaboration. It enhances 21st-century learning through ICT, multimedia, and online platforms.

The Importance of Empowerment Technology

Digital Literacy

Develops the ability to use modern tools effectively and responsibly in both learning and teaching.

Innovation

Encourages creative approaches to problem-solving using technology-driven solutions.

Future Readiness

Prepares students for a technology-oriented workplace by building ICT and critical thinking skills.
